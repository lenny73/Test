From Mountaintop to Countertop

Coffee Guru is devoted to connecting coffee-lovers with the finest roasters in the world. A guru is one who teaches or enlightens, and it is the Coffee Guru’s goal to provide a clear passage to that most sacred nectar of enlightenment - coffee We roast in small batches at our facility in Camarillo, CA, where we find the roast that best suits the beans from each origin and captures what makes each country’s coffee unique. In other words, we don’t do anything halfway.
